About TLAPS

Built by I3 Enterprise Inc.

I3 Enterprise Inc., founded in 2015 and headquartered in Walnut, CA 91789, is a US-based e-commerce and wholesale distribution company with over a decade of experience operating on Amazon Vendor Central, eBay, and major online marketplaces.

In 2026, I3 launched TLAPS — a commercial food service disposables brand — in strategic partnership with one of the largest food service product importers in the United States. Our supply chain partner brings over 25 years of industry expertise, operates a major warehouse and distribution center in Southern California, and maintains direct relationships with manufacturing facilities worldwide.

TLAPS combines I3's proven marketplace expertise with our partner's deep food service supply chain — delivering commercial-grade quality at competitive pricing to restaurants, caterers, food trucks, delis, institutional buyers, and home cooks nationwide. Every product ships with Amazon Prime.
